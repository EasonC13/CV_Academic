CV (PDF)

Eason is a senior at National Taiwan Normal University (NTNU). The meaning of his name 'Eason' originated from his Chinese name 怡升 (I-Sheng), which is moving onward and upward with a joyful mind.

He is a passionate researcher. As an undergraduate student, he participated in several research projects and wrote seven papers in AI, Human-Computer Interaction, Education, and Blockchain.

Moreover, He loves to use programming skills and creativity to solve problems. Currently, he serve as Research Assistant at school and Blockchain R&D Engineer at Cohart.co.

Eason plans to start his Ph.D. in US or UK in 2023 Fall. Now he's looking for program, lab, professor, and scholarship. If you have a potential opportunity, don't hesitate to get in touch with him. at eason.tw.chen@gmail.com
